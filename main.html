TextRank

TextRank is an unsupervised graph-based algorithm for extractive text summarization and keyword extraction. First a cosine similarity matrix is constructed between each sentences and each sentence is represented as nodes in a graph , with edges representing relationships based on cosine similarity. The algorithm assigns weights to these edges and applies the PageRank algorithm to iteratively update the importance scores of nodes. In the context of extractive summarization, sentences with higher importance scores are selected to form the summary.
